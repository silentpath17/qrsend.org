Cryptocurrency Wallet Guide

Learn how to create and use cryptocurrency wallets safely

Bitcoin (BTC)

1. Choose a Wallet

Recommended wallets:

  • Hardware Wallet (Most Secure): Ledger, Trezor
  • Mobile Wallet: BlueWallet, Electrum
  • Desktop Wallet: Bitcoin Core, Electrum

2. Download and Install

Download your chosen wallet from the official website only. Never trust third-party sources.

3. Create Your Wallet

Follow the wallet's setup process. You'll receive a recovery phrase (seed phrase) - write this down and store it safely offline.

Never share your recovery phrase with anyone. Anyone with access to it can access your funds.

4. Find Your Address

In your wallet, look for "Receive" or "Get Address". This is your public Bitcoin address that you can share to receive funds.

Monero (XMR)

1. Choose a Wallet

Recommended wallets:

  • Desktop: Monero GUI, Monero CLI
  • Mobile: Cake Wallet, Monerujo

2. Download and Install

Download from getmonero.org or your device's official app store.

3. Create Your Wallet

Follow the setup process. Save your recovery phrase securely.

4. Find Your Address

Look for "Receive" or "Address" in your wallet to find your Monero address.

Ethereum (ETH)

1. Choose a Wallet

Recommended wallets:

  • Browser Extension: MetaMask
  • Mobile: Trust Wallet, MetaMask Mobile
  • Hardware: Ledger, Trezor

2. Install Your Wallet

For MetaMask: Install the browser extension from metamask.io

3. Create Your Wallet

Follow the setup process and securely store your recovery phrase.

4. Find Your Address

Click on your account in MetaMask to copy your Ethereum address.

Security Tips

1. Backup Your Recovery Phrase

Write it down on paper and store it in a secure location. Never store it digitally.

2. Use Strong Passwords

Create unique, strong passwords for your wallets.

3. Enable 2FA When Available

Use two-factor authentication for additional security.

4. Keep Your Software Updated

Regularly update your wallet software to the latest version.
